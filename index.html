Projects

Barbells and Brews is an innovative competition series in the sport of weightlifting. Barbells and Brews breaks tradition and creates an atmosphere unlike any other weightlifting competition by having loud music, entertaining announcers, and amazing coffee and beer. In this project, I brought to life a website with a carousel to showcase some of the photos from past events that showed the personality of the event. Other sections included the mission, staff, and testimonials. The main page is a fullly responsive scrolling single page site made with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. Other pages use PHP and MySQL to bring results and records to the page. Technologies include HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, and MySQL. -Barbells and Brews, L.L.C.
The Weightlifting Scoop is one of the most popular podcasts in the sport of weightlifting. The Weightlifting Scoop site is made with Wix and showcases several pages including a shopping cart. All images were made in GIMP, and all audio is edited in Audacity to fit the personality of the podcast. Technologies involved in this project include GIMP, Spreaker, Audacity, iTunes, Wix, customized HTML and CSS. -The Weightlifting Scoop, L.L.C.
Picture Eliminator is a small project that focuses on voting based on the process of elimination. People tend to vote differently when voting for their favorite out of a large population than when they vote based on the process of eliminating their least favorite out of a large population. This small project focuses on removing and adding elements to HTML using JavaScript.
Christmas Countdown is a small project that focuses on using the date functionality in JavaScript. When the current date changes relative to the goal date the HTML updates to display the countdown. This small project was done based on a further idea to use countdowns to help people reach their goals.
